CHULA is a novel Custom Heuristic Uncertainty-guided Loss for Accurate segmentation and detection of Thai land title deeds. It combines:

  • Class-balanced cross-entropy,
  • Aleatoric uncertainty modeling, and
  • Domain-specific heuristic priors.

This loss function is model-agnostic and can be plugged into any detection or segmentation model (e.g., YOLOv12, DeepLabv3+) to improve performance on noisy, ambiguous, and structure-rich documents.
